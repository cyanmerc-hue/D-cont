Benefits
Simple list of what you get as a D-CONT user. Core trust points are always visible; rules/details are expandable.
Benefits for NEW customers
1) D-CONT never holds your money
You pay directly to other members using UPI. The app does not take or keep your money.
Where you’ll see this: Home (top card) and Payments (near Pay).
2) Start saving with just ₹500
You can start with small monthly amounts. Entry is easy for first-time users.
Where you’ll see this: Home and Groups list.
3) Groups are created by D-CONT
You don’t have to trust random people creating groups. Groups are managed by the platform.
Where you’ll see this: Groups tab and Join confirmation.
4) No payments in the first month
You get time to understand the group before you start monthly contributions.
Where you’ll see this: Group details and Join confirmation.
5) Clear rules, no surprises (View Group Rules)
Everything is fixed and visible: monthly amount, members, and duration. This keeps things predictable.
Where you’ll see this: Group details → Rules section.
Benefits for EXISTING / ACTIVE customers
6) You know exactly who to pay and when
Payments tab shows the monthly task clearly so you don’t get confused or miss a payment.
Where you’ll see this: Payments tab.
7) Trust score rewards honest members (How score works)
Paying on time improves your Trust Score. Higher trust can unlock more access.
Where you’ll see this: Profile (Trust Score) and member badges.
8) Late payers are automatically restricted
Missed/late payments reduce trust and can restrict future access. This protects honest members.
Where you’ll see this: Home safety note and Group rules.
9) Security deposit protects the group
If you request early payout, a refundable security deposit may be required. It shows commitment and reduces risk for the group.
Where you’ll see this: Group details → Deposit section (only when applicable).
10) Deposit is returned as you continue paying
If you keep paying as agreed, the deposit can be returned after verification as per rules.
Where you’ll see this: Group deposit status and Profile deposit summary.
11) Earn rewards by inviting trusted people
You earn ₹10 app-fee credit when your friend joins a group and pays their first app fee.
Where you’ll see this: Profile → Invite & Earn and Rewards tab.
12) Referral rewards reduce your app fee
Your ₹30 monthly app fee can become ₹20 or ₹0 using credits.
Where you’ll see this: Payments (app fee) and Profile credits.
13) No paperwork, no bank visits
Everything works using UPI. No office visits.
Where you’ll see this: Home and setup screens.
14) You stay in control
You choose groups and monthly amounts. You can stop joining new ones anytime (as per rules).
Where you’ll see this: Profile account info and Groups.
Golden UX rule
Trust benefits are always visible. Rules are expandable. Details are in dropdowns/tooltips.
